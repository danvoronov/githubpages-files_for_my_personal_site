I have been exploring Kyiv for a long time. I used to find many interesting channels or just single videos on YouTube about **urbex** and sneaking into places in Kyiv, in Ukraine, and later around the world.

Almost all of them have been deleted and blocked. The **Gifgas** channel is somehow still holding on, though he is preparing for it to be taken down. He mostly films in the EU, but now he has reached Ukraine too.

Since 2022, the number of urban explorers here has decreased significantly, as the measures taken when caught are completely different now.

[https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=NfUGFB-oOoc](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=NfUGFB-oOoc)

It is interesting to watch how a foreigner who doesn't live here sees **a damaged Kyiv**.

He shows how unusual it is for him to hear air raid sirens, see city blocks completely disconnected from the grid, and sit in an apartment with no power. He also films the noisy generators at every kiosk, houses with blown-out walls and windows, and the makeshift memorial to the fallen heroes.
